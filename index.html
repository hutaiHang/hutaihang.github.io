About Me

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the College of Information Sciences and Technology (IST) at Penn State University (PSU). I am co-advised by Prof. Suhang Wang and Prof. Xiang Zhang. I also work very closely with Prof. Lei Feng.

My recent research interests lie in building effective and trustworthy AI foundation models (such as large language models and large vision-language models).
