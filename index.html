About Me

I am currently a second-year MS student at Tianjin Key Laboratory of Visual Computing and Intelligent Perception (VCIP, the lab is lead by Ming-Ming Cheng), Nankai University, advised by Prof. Yaxing Wang. My research interests lie in the acceleration of generative models and semantic alignment, such as accelerating diffusion models without training and enhancing the semantic alignment of diffusion models with text prompts, as well as focusing on safer generative models.
