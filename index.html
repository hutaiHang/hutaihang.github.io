About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in the College of Information Sciences and Technology (IST) at Penn State University (PSU). I am co-advised by Prof. Suhang Wang and Prof. Xiang Zhang. I also work very closely with Prof. Lei Feng.

My general research interests lie in machine learning and data mining, with a particular emphasis on building effective and trustworthy AI foundation models (such as large language models, vision-language models, and diffusion models).
